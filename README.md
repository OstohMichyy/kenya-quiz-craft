#  StudyForge

**Forge Knowledge. Master Tomorrow.**

StudyForge is an AI-powered educational platform designed to help students and teachers generate high-quality quizzes, assessments, and revision materials instantly.

Built with a strong focus on the **Kenyan CBC/CBE curriculum**, **KNEC standards**, **KCSE preparation**, and **university-level learning**, StudyForge transforms any topic or pasted notes into meaningful practice questions and explanations.

---

##  Features

###  AI Quiz Generator

Generate quizzes from any topic in seconds.

* Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
* Short Answer Questions
* Essay Questions
* True/False Questions
* Fill-in-the-Blanks
* Structured Examination Questions

---

###  Paragraph-to-Quiz Generator

Paste any notes, textbook content, article, or revision material and let StudyForge:

* Extract key concepts
* Generate questions automatically
* Produce answer keys
* Create comprehension-style exercises
* Generate revision material

---

###  Difficulty Levels

Choose the challenge level that matches your learning goals.

####  Easy

* Basic understanding
* Recall questions
* Beginner-friendly explanations

####  Medium

* Standard KCSE-level questions
* Concept application
* Analytical thinking

####  Difficult

* Advanced problem-solving
* Higher-order thinking
* University-level depth

###  Mathematics Step-by-Step Solutions

For Mathematics questions, StudyForge provides:

* Detailed workings

* Marking-scheme approach
* Student-friendly breakdowns
